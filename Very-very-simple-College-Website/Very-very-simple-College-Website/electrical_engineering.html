Electronic & Telecomm Engineering


Course Overview

Electronic & Telecommunication Engineering is a field of engineering that focuses on designing, testing, and supervising the manufacture of electronic and telecommunication equipment. It covers a wide range of technologies, including digital electronics, analog electronics, signal processing, telecommunications systems, and more.



Skills Developed

  • Proficiency in electronic circuit design
  • Knowledge of communication systems
  • Problem-solving abilities
  • Understanding of signal processing techniques
  • Project management skills


Career Opportunities

Graduates of Electronic & Telecommunication Engineering can pursue careers in various industries, including telecommunications, electronics manufacturing, information technology, aerospace, defense, and more. Job roles may include Electronics Engineer, Telecommunication Engineer, Network Engineer, Systems Engineer, and Research Scientist.
