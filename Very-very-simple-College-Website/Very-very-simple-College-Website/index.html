SHREEYASH COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING & TECHNOLOGY (POLYTECHNIC)

Shreeyash Polytechnic provides a comprehensive education in engineering, emphasizing mathematics, computation, and the physical and life sciences.
Our dedicated faculty and supportive staff, along with a vibrant student community, make SYPOLY an institute of global importance. Students are encouraged to apply their learning through projects, internships, and research.

Our Diploma Program

We offer a range of engineering diploma programs to prepare students for successful careers.

Electrical Engineering

Our Electrical Engineering program covers the fundamentals of electrical systems, electronics, and power generation, preparing students for dynamic careers in the industry.

Telecom Engineering

The Telecom Engineering program focuses on communication technologies, network systems, and signal processing, equipping students with the skills needed for the telecommunications sector.

Applied Science

The Applied Science program integrates practical scientific knowledge with engineering applications, providing a strong foundation for various technical careers.

AI and ML

This program specializes in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, teaching students the latest technologies and methodologies in AI research and applications.

Computer Engineering

The Computer Engineering program combines principles of computer science and electrical engineering to develop hardware and software solutions for modern computing challenges.

Civil Engineering

The Civil Engineering program provides comprehensive knowledge in the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure projects, preparing students for a career in civil engineering.

Mechanical Engineering

Our Mechanical Engineering program covers the principles of mechanics, materials, and energy to design and manufacture mechanical systems, preparing students for diverse engineering roles.

Our facilities

Facilities provide for Students by Shreeyash Pratishthan

Green Campus

The college prides itself on its green campus, which is adorned with lush gardens, trees, and eco-friendly infrastructure. The emphasis on sustainability is evident through various initiatives such as rainwater harvesting, solar power installations, and waste recycling programs.

Computer Center

The college's computer center is a hub of technological resources, offering an extensive range of computing facilities to students and staff.

Digital Classroom

Our digital classrooms are equipped with advanced technological tools to facilitate modern learning methods. These classrooms feature high-definition projectors, interactive whiteboards, and robust internet connectivity, allowing for multimedia presentations, online resources, and real-time collaboration between students and teachers, both in-person and remotely.

Canteen

The objective of the Canteen and meal service is to protect by reducing the risk of foodborne illness, with proper sanitary conditions.

Auditorium

The college is having a huge indoor auditorium with a professionally-deployed acoustic system and a seating capacity of about 200 people.

Hostel

hostel consists of a separate mess, administrative office, warden quarter. In addition to well furnished rooms.

Library

It offers a quiet and conducive environment for study and research, with comfortable seating and access to computers and the internet for academic purposes.

Transportation

The college provides a reliable transportation system with a fleet of buses covering various routes across the city and nearby areas. This ensures safe and comfortable travel for students and staff, with timely schedules that accommodate the college timings and special events.

Sports Ground

The college boasts an expansive sports ground equipped with facilities for a variety of sports including cricket, football, volleyball, and athletics.
